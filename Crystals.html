Seashells Collector!

Rules of the game:

  • You will given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four seashells below. By clicking on a seashell you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each seashell is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each seashell.

Good luck!
